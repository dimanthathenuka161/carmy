Privacy Policy

Learn how Ceylon Wheels collects, uses, and safeguards your personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ceylon Wheels. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information collected during vehicle bookings, customer support communications, and admin portal updates.

2. Information We Collect

To complete vehicle bookings and fulfill legal requirements under Sri Lankan transport laws, we collect:

  • 🔹 Identity Details: Full Name, National Identity Card (NIC) number, or Passport Details.
  • 🔹 Permits & Licenses: Driving License number (required for self-drive options).
  • 🔹 Contact Info: Email address, mobile telephone number.
  • 🔹 Rental Selections: Pickup and dropoff hubs, driver options, dates, and vehicle models.
  • 🔹 Payment Details: Mock card numbers, expiry dates, and billing preferences (all processed in secure client-side simulations).

3. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is strictly used to process booking confirmations, compute rental invoice pricing, verify driving eligibility for self-drive rentals, assign hired local drivers, and present business reports on our administrative panels.

4. Data Protection & Security

We employ modern browser-level local storage encryption structures. Because all calculations and bookings run on your client-side environment (pure HTML/CSS/JS architecture), no financial credentials or copies of your identity documents are transmitted over standard remote networks. Your billing inputs are simulated locally.

5. Customer Rights

You hold full rights to view, request changes to, or delete your booking details. Since your data is preserved in your local browser environment, clearing your browser cookies and site database cache will immediately and permanently erase all saved bookings and custom vehicle entries.
