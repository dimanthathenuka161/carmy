About Ceylon Wheels

Learn more about Sri Lanka's leading digital vehicle rental gateway.

Our Mission

Founded in Colombo, **Ceylon Wheels** is on a mission to redefine the vehicle rental experience in Sri Lanka. We close the gap between traditional rental companies and digital customers by offering a modern web interface, transparent LKR pricing, and an extensively maintained vehicle fleet.

Whether you are a local resident needing a temporary ride for a wedding, a corporate team traveling to a regional office, or an international tourist embarking on a tour to Ella, Kandy, and Galle—we ensure your drive is smooth, safe, and premium.

Ceylon Wheels Standards

  • ✔
    Rigorous Inspections: Every vehicle goes through an 80-point safety and cleanliness audit before dispatch.
  • ✔
    Fair Refundable Deposits: Low deposits in LKR which are credited back within 24 hours of vehicle return.
  • ✔
    Driver Support: Settle driver salaries directly with bookings. Driver meals & lodging are fully managed.

Sri Lankan Car Rental Guidelines

🪪 Driving Licenses

Sri Lankan residents must hold a valid Class B driving license. International tourists require an International Driving Permit (IDP) or a temporary endorsement from the AAC in Colombo to drive.

💸 LKR Pricing & Deposits

In compliance with local standards, all rates are charged in LKR. A refundable security deposit is block-charged on your card. The deposit is completely waived if you hire a driver.

🗺️ Driver working terms

Driver standard shifts are 8 hours (8 AM - 6 PM). Driver lodging is managed by us. Additional hours cost Rs. 500/hour, which is paid directly to the driver at the end of the trip.
